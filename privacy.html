Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

LIFT is a personal fitness tracking application for private use. This policy explains how data is handled.

Data Collected

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Body weight entries
  • WHOOP health data (recovery, sleep, strain) — only when you explicitly connect your WHOOP account

How Data Is Used

All data is used solely to display your personal fitness history and progress within the app. It is never sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Data Storage

Workout and body weight data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), a secure cloud database. WHOOP credentials are stored locally in your browser and are never transmitted to any third party other than WHOOP's official API.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — database storage
  • WHOOP API — health and recovery data (optional, requires your explicit authorization)

Contact

This app is for personal use only. For questions, contact the app owner directly.
